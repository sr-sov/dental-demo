# Project Creative Brief: "Prairie Oak Dental Studio" (Calgary, Alberta)

This document represents the operational notes, brand identity, and client intake details compiled from our onboarding meeting with the practice owner. It is written completely in business-focused, non-technical language to guide our copy, design strategy, and asset layout.

---

## 1. Practice Identity & About Us

### The Owner’s Story
Prairie Oak Dental Studio is a locally owned, modern dental clinic located in south Calgary. The founder, Dr. Sarah Al-Hussaini, opened the practice after working for years in large, high-volume corporate clinics. She felt patients were being treated like numbers on a spreadsheet, rushed through assembly-line appointments that maximized billing but ignored patient comfort.

### Who We Are
We are a warm, community-focused boutique clinic that treats oral health as a long-term relationship, not a series of transactional procedures. Our clinic is designed to look and feel like a modern Alberta home—featuring warm wood textures, natural light, and a fireplace in the reception area—to strip away the sterile, intimidating atmosphere traditionally associated with dental offices. 

---

## 2. Core Value Proposition

We do not compete on being the lowest-priced clinic in Calgary; we charge standard Alberta Dental Fee Guide rates. Instead, we compete on **unrivaled patient comfort, transparency, and administrative convenience.**

### What Sets Us Apart
*   **Anxiety-Free Comfort Menu:** We proactively offer patients complimentary comfort options during treatment, including weighted blankets, noise-canceling headphones, streaming entertainment on ceiling-mounted screens, and mild sedation options for nervous appointments.
*   **True Direct Billing (Assignment of Benefits):** We handle all the insurance paperwork on behalf of the patient. We bill Alberta Blue Cross, Sun Life, Manulife, Canada Life, and all major providers directly. The patient only pays their specific co-pay out-of-pocket on the day of treatment.
*   **Same-Day Emergency Guarantee:** We reserve dedicated emergency slots in our schedule every single day. If a patient calls us with sudden oral pain, a broken tooth, or a lost filling, we guarantee to see them the exact same day to get them out of discomfort.

---

## 3. Target Market & Ideal Patient Profiles

Our primary market consists of suburban residents, working parents, and busy young professionals living or working within a 15-minute radius of our south Calgary location. 

### Key Patient Personas
*   **The Busy Professional:** Wants highly efficient care, online scheduling that respects their time without delays, and instant clarity on whether their corporate insurance matches our direct billing system.
*   **The High-Anxiety Resident:** Has avoided the dentist for 5 to 10 years due to a past traumatic experience. They are deeply self-conscious about their current oral health and need an empathetic, judgment-free environment to get back on track.
*   **The Emergency Patient:** A local resident experiencing sudden, severe dental pain. They aren't looking to browse a deep website—they are on a mobile device looking for an immediate phone number and a promise of a same-day appointment.

## 4. Service Categories

To keep the layout clean and highly scannable for users, we group our clinical offerings into three consumer-friendly categories, keeping medical terminology to an absolute minimum.

### 1. General Dentistry & Prevention
*   Comprehensive oral checkups, dental exams, and digital X-rays.
*   Professional cleanings, scaling, and personalized hygiene plans.
*   Pediatric checkups designed to create a fun, positive foundation for children.

### 2. Restorative & Same-Day Emergency Care
*   Natural-looking, tooth-coloured (composite) fillings.
*   Root canal therapy and immediate pain relief protocols.
*   Durable dental crowns, bridges, and implant restorations.

### 3. Cosmetic Transformations & Aligners
*   Professional in-office laser teeth whitening and custom take-home kits.
*   Porcelain veneers to correct chips, minor gaps, or uneven spacing.
*   Invisalign® clear aligner therapy for subtle adult orthodontic corrections.

## 5. Visual Identity & Brand Guidelines

The overall digital presence must project clinical excellence mixed with approachable warmth. **It must never look cold, stark, or institutional.**

*   **The Color Palette:** Clean dental white backgrounds paired with a primary deep slate blue for high-end professionalism, supported by soft earth tones (such as warm taupe or soft prairie sage) to lower heart rates. A vibrant orange or gold must be reserved exclusively for high-visibility action buttons.
*   **Photography Style:** Real human smiles, natural lighting, and lifestyle images showcasing comfortable environments. Absolutely no generic, unnaturally bright stock photos of models with impossibly white teeth.
*   **Typography:** Soft, clean, contemporary sans-serif fonts that are easy to read across all mobile viewports and devices.

---

## 6. Website Goals & Conversion Requirements

The new landing page must act as an automated extension of our front desk. It needs to give users immediate answers to their administrative questions and guide them seamlessly toward booking.

*   **The Primary Call to Action:** A clear, high-visibility button reading **"Request an Appointment"** or **"New Patient Intake"** that anchors the layout.
*   **The Mobile First Priority:** A sticky, prominent **"Call Now for Emergency Care"** floating interaction bar that activates immediately on mobile screens.
*   **Upfront Financial Trust:** Visual badges placed high on the page displaying: *“Direct Insurance Billing Accepted,” “Following the Alberta Fee Guide,”* and *“Free On-Site Parking.”*
*   **Social Proof Integration:** A dedicated section highlighting our 5-star local reputation, featuring 3 to 4 specific text reviews from Calgary residents praising our gentle approach and clear communication.
