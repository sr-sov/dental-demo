# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Suburban residents, working parents, and busy young professionals living or working in south Calgary. Key personas include:
1. The Busy Professional: Wants highly efficient care, online scheduling, and instant insurance clarity.
2. The High-Anxiety Resident: Avoided the dentist for 5-10 years due to trauma; needs an empathetic, judgment-free environment.
3. The Emergency Patient: In sudden pain, looking on a mobile device for an immediate phone number and same-day care guarantee.

## Product Purpose
A premier boutique dental landing page that acts as an automated extension of the front desk. It answers administrative and financial questions upfront (direct insurance billing, fee guide) and guides users seamlessly toward scheduling via a high-visibility CTA ("Request an appointment").

## Brand Personality
Voice & Tone: Empathetic, approachable, transparent, warm, and highly professional.
3-word personality: Residential Warmth, Clinical Excellence, Anxiety-Free.
Emotional goals: Strip away clinical anxiety by evoking a feeling of walking into a modern, cozy Alberta home (wood textures, natural light, fireplace).

## Anti-references
- Cold, stark, sterile, or institutional dental clinics.
- Generic, unnaturally bright stock photos of models with impossibly white teeth.
- Flat spacing and monotonous card grids.
- Loud, overstimulating, or hyperactive animations.

## Design Principles
1. **Cozy Residential Sanctuary:** Emulate the look and feel of a modern Alberta home to lower heart rates and remove clinical dread.
2. **Transparent Administrative Peace:** Proactively display direct insurance billing and standard Fee Guide rates high on the page to build immediate trust.
3. **Frictionless Action Pathways:** Ensure the primary "Request an Appointment" call to action is highly visible and that a sticky emergency bar is instantly reachable on mobile viewports.
4. **Authentic Calgary Representation:** Use real, natural human photography and genuine testimonials to build localized social proof rather than generic SaaS cliches.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict AA/AAA accessibility compliance for all colors and typography (especially for anxious patients scanning copy).
- Large, clear, high-readability text with high typography contrast.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` settings.
